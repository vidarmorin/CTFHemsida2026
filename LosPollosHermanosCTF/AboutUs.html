The story of Los Pollos Hermanos

At Los Pollos Hermanos, we have been serving the most delicious and crispy fried chicken in the Southwest for over 20 years. Founded by Gustavo Fring, our family business has grown to become a beloved institution in the community.

At Los Pollos Hermanos, we pride ourselves on serving the most delicious and crispy fried chicken in the Southwest. Our secret blend of spices and our commitment to quality ingredients make us a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
